
Privacy Policy for VScanner AI
Last updated: April 17, 2026

VScanner AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by VScanner AI.

1. Information We Collect
A. Camera Access
VScanner AI requires access to your device's camera to enable the document scanning and OCR (Optical Character Recognition) features. We do not store or transmit any images or video from your camera to our servers. All image processing (scanning and text extraction) happens locally on your device.

B. Storage Access
VScanner AI requires access to your device's storage to save scanned documents, images, and extracted text files. We only access files created by the app or files you explicitly select to import into the app.

C. Analytical Data
We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app's performance and user experience. This data does not contain any personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the services offered by VScanner AI.

Camera data is used only for real-time scanning and OCR.
Storage data is used only to manage your local document library.
3. Data Security
Since VScanner AI processes all your documents locally on your device, your sensitive information never leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to share it (e.g., via email or third-party cloud apps).

4. Third-Party Services
VScanner AI uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data (such as your device's advertising ID) to show you relevant ads. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data.

5. Children's Privacy
VScanner AI does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
